MHacks 6

September 11th-13th

University of Michigan

North Campus

Apply

Come together for 36 hours with the brightest developers, designers, and creators the world has to offer and make something amazing.

As a hacker, you won’t be breaking into databases or security systems. You and your team will be applying your knowledge in app development, web development, hardware, etc in order to create innovative solutions to an existing problem.

No matter the skill level, every hacker has the opportunity to grow and learn. This year, in going back to the core elements of a successful hackathon- mentorship, community, and creation-, MHacks will provide an environment where this growth happens naturally.

About

With MLH’s hardware lab as well as our own hardware, MHacks 6 will have all the resources you need to create something you’re proud of. To help you step out of your comfort zone, we have an awesome lineup of sponsor-led tech talks and workshops where hackers dabble in new technologies. Dream big, hack bigger.

You, the hackers, are the most important part of MHacks. We don’t want you to build just hacks; we want you to build relationships with the incredible people around you, because it's the snowball fights, dance parties, and midnight Insomnia cookies that you'll remember the most.

No one should be afraid to ask for help. If you plan on working with a specific technology, join one of our cortices — spaces where people hack on the same type of tech, together. This year, we’re offering six different cortices: iOS, Android, Web Development, Virtual Reality/Game Development, Hardware, and Social Impact. Mentors in each cortex will be there to help you out.

FAQ

Q: When and where is MHacks?
A: MHacks 6 will open the doors for hacker registration on the evening of Friday, September 11th on the University of Michigan's North Campus. More details will become available as we near the event.

Q: What should I bring?
A: Bring your laptop, phone, student ID, toiletries, water bottle, a change of clothes, and a sense of adventure. We'll do our best to get you anything else you might need.

Q: What should I bring?
A: Your laptop, a sleeping bag, and a thirst for adventure.

Q: What can't I bring?
A: Weapons of any kind, drugs, alcohol, and dank memes.

Q: What can I hack on?
A: We'll be providing lots of awesome hardware to hack on, courtesy of our sponsors. Stay tuned to this page and MHacks social media for updates.
